# Materials
* 35x25.4 mm Copper tubing.
* 1 25.4 Copper tubing cap.
* 40x10 mm Allthread Hollow Threaded Tube Electrical Lamp Socket Rod with 2 nuts.
* 1 10mm cotton lamp cord.
* 1 Empty Gerber flask with cap.
* 1 10mm drill bit.
* Hand drill.
